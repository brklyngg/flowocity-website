Finance, Reimagined for the AI SaaS Era

We turn AI-era complexity into clarity - giving founders investor-grade visibility from day one.

Hybrid Revenue Models

We separate committed ARR from usage-based revenue, giving you and your investors a true picture of predictable vs. variable income.

Dynamic Scenario Planning

AI SaaS growth can be unpredictable. We model usage-driven adoption alongside recurring revenue so you can plan with confidence - even through volatility.

COGS Management

Compute and model-inference costs can quietly erode margins. We isolate these costs to report both GAAP Gross Margin and "ex-compute" Gross Margin, revealing the true health of your software business.

Scalable GTM

We track human + AI productivity across your funnel — linking conversion, automation impact, and CAC efficiency to show what's driving scalable growth.

Operating Leverage

We measure how human and AI productivity scale together - tracking ARR per FTE to show how efficient systems and high-impact hires combine to create true operating leverage.

Capital Efficiency

We track Burn Multiple - how much capital you invest to generate each dollar of net new ARR - giving you clear visibility into the efficiency and sustainability of your growth.
