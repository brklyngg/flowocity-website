Pricing varies based on engagement scope—contact us to discuss your specific situation and receive a tailored proposal.

Because we work as an integrated team with proven collaboration, we can onboard faster than individual fractional consultants. We design engagements with transition in mind. All deliverables, processes, and documentation are built to be transferable. When you're ready to hire full-time finance leadership, we support the transition by training your new hire, transferring knowledge, and ensuring continuity. Many clients maintain a fractional relationship even after hiring full-time CFOs for specialized support.

(3) Our deliverables are transparent and collaborative—you own everything we build, not black-box consulting outputs.

While we specialize in SaaS and AI startups (where our Greenhouse experience is most directly applicable), we've also worked with CPG companies (The Farmer's Dog), e-commerce, MarTech, and HR Tech. Our core expertise is scaling venture-backed startups through rapid growth—if that describes your company, we'd love to discuss how we can help.

Our team model provides built-in continuity. With three complementary roles (CFO, Controller, Assistant Controller), there's always coverage. We maintain shared documentation and processes, so any team member can step in seamlessly. This is a key advantage over individual fractional CFOs, who create bottlenecks when unavailable.
